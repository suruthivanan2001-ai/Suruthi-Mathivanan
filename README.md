## Unique Features

### 🌼 Bloom Score™
A holistic financial wellness score (0–100) broken into 4 sub-dimensions:
- **Saving** — savings rate vs income
- **Budgeting** — budget adherence
- **Goals** — goal progress velocity
- **Habits** — consistency of check-ins and journaling

This replaces a single scary number with a growth narrative.

### 🌿 Money Pulse™
Users self-report their financial mood: Thriving / Steady / Stressed.
Bloom responds with a contextually appropriate, empathetic insight — not a generic alert.

### 📓 Money Journal
A private weekly 2-minute reflection. **Not tracked, not analyzed.**
Reduces the surveillance anxiety that makes people disengage from finance apps.

## Design Decisions

**Palette:** Warm cream + sage green — calm and organic, not clinical blue or harsh red.
Terracotta replaces red for over-budget states — still attention-catching, far less alarming.

**Typography:** Fraunces (serif display) pairs with Plus Jakarta Sans (body).
Fraunces feels editorial and trustworthy; unusual for finance which makes it memorable.

**Language:** No "warning", "failed", "exceeded". Over-budget = "over by ₹X". Progress not punishment.

**Navigation order:** Home (overview) → Spending (drill) → Goals (future) → Insights (reflection) → Profile (settings).
Mirrors a natural mental journey from "where am I?" to "where am I going?" to "how am I feeling?".
